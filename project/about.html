About

I’m a digital designer specialising in branding, visual identities and web experiences. My work is driven by a strong belief that good design should feel both intuitive and intentional — balancing aesthetics with functionality.

Over the years, I’ve worked with a range of clients, from early-stage startups to established brands, helping them define how they look, communicate and exist digitally. Whether it’s building a brand from the ground up or refining an existing identity, my focus is always on clarity, consistency and long-term value.

My approach combines structured thinking with a flexible and collaborative process. I care about the details — from typography and layout to interaction and performance — ensuring every project feels cohesive across all touchpoints.

Common questions

What do you actually do as a digital designer?

I design brands and digital experiences — from visual identities to fully built websites. My work focuses on creating clear, structured and visually refined solutions that help brands communicate more effectively.

What kind of projects do you enjoy working on?

I enjoy working on projects where design plays a central role in shaping how a brand is perceived — whether that’s building a new identity, refining an existing one, or translating it into a strong digital experience.

What is your approach to design?

My approach is rooted in clarity, structure and intention. I focus on building systems rather than isolated visuals, ensuring that each design decision contributes to a cohesive and scalable outcome.

What does a typical project look like?

Most projects start with understanding the context and defining clear goals. From there, I move into concept development, design exploration and refinement, followed by execution and implementation — often in close collaboration with the client.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Yes — I work with clients worldwide and structure projects to work smoothly across time zones, using async communication and clear milestones.

What tools and technologies do you use?

I primarily work with Figma for design and Framer for building websites. Depending on the project, I also use Adobe Creative Suite and other tools to support branding and visual development.

Are you available for new projects?

Availability depends on current workload, but I’m always open to hearing about new opportunities, collaborations or interesting ideas.
